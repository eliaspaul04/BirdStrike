Game description

Welcome to our thrilling game, in which you must fly a jet over the skies while avoiding a swarm of birds in order to survive. You lose a life when you collide with a bird, and the game ends when your life supply runs out. Earn points for the length of your flight while competing against the clock. For what duration can you endure the avian maze? In this intense battle, aim for the top spot on the leaderboard!

Game Rules

  • Hit "Play" to start; the plane moves automatically.
  • Control the plane with up arrow key to ascend, and the down arrow key to descend.
  • The plane's speed is fixed.
  • Begin with three lives; losing them ends the game.
  • Complete your mission within 20 seconds for victory.
